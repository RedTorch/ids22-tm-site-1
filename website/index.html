Models for Interaction Design

For the first machine learning model, we thought models that are implementable for games would be an interesting topic. Currently, there are games which detect your body with a camera such as Kinect from Microsoft, but it is yet to prevail in our society. We made a few models that detects our body with a skeleton from the "Pose Project" in the Google Teachable Machine.
